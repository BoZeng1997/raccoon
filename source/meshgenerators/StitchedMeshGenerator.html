StitchedMeshGenerator

Overview

The StitchedMeshGenerator object allows for multiple mesh files to be "stitched" together to form a single mesh for use in a simulation. This generator will combine overlapping nodes and turn overlapping external sides into internal sides to form a connected mesh.
