ADComputeGreenLagrangeStrain

Compute a Green-Lagrange strain.

The Green-Lagrange strain is a non-linear total strain

E=\frac12 \left(F^TF-1\right)

Where is the deformation gradient tensor. It can be combined with ADComputeLinearElasticStress to compute a second Piola-Kirchhoff stress (use the ADStressDivergence kernel on the undisplaced mesh). This combination is called a St. Venant-Kirchhoff hyper elasticity model.
