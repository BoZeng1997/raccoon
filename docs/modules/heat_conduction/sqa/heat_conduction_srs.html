Heat Conduction System Requirement Specification

Introduction

The SRS for Heat Conduction describes the system functional and non-functional requirements that describe the expected interactions that the software shall provide.

Dependencies

The Heat Conduction application is developed using MOOSE and is based on various modules, as such the SRS for Heat Conduction is dependent upon the following documents.

  • Framework System Requirements Specification

Requirements

The following is a complete list for all the functional requirements for Heat Conduction.

  • heat_conduction: Ad Heat Conduction
  • F4.1.1AD heat conduction and the Jacobian shall be beautiful
  • heat_conduction: Generate Radiation Patch
  • F4.2.1The system shall be able to divide a sideset into patches for more accurate radiative transfer modeling.
  • F4.2.2The system shall be able to use linear partitioner for subdividing sidesets into patches.
  • F4.2.3The system shall be able to use centroid partitioner for subdividing sidesets into patches.
  • F4.2.4The system shall error when centroid partitioner is used but centroid_partitioner_direction is not provided.
  • heat_conduction: Gray Lambert Radiator

  • F4.3.2The system shall check consistency of boundary and view factor entries
  • F4.3.3The system shall check consistency of fixed_boundary_temperatures and fixed_temperature_boundary entries
  • F4.3.4The system shall check consistency of boundary and fixed_temperature_boundary entries
  • F4.3.5The system shall check consistency of boundary and adiabatic_boundary entries
  • F4.3.6The system shall check consistency of the view_factors entry
  • F4.3.7The system shall check consistency of the view_factors entry
  • F4.3.8The system shall compute radiative transfer between gray Lambert surfaces
  • F4.3.9The system shall allow coupling radiative transfer between gray Lambert surfaces to solving heat conduction
  • F4.3.10The system shall allow reconstructing the spatial distribution of the emission component on a radiation boundary via the T4 law
  • F4.3.11The system shall compute radiative transfer between gray Lambert surfaces when the view factors are provided by a userobject
  • F4.3.12The system shall compute radiative transfer between gray Lambert surfaces when the view factors are provided by a userobject
  • heat_conduction: Heat Conduction
  • F4.4.1MOOSE shall compute the heat transfer across small gaps for supported FEM orders and quadratures, moving interfaces, and non-matching meshes.
  • F4.4.2MOOSE shall compute the heat transfer across small gaps for supported FEM orders and quadratures, moving interfaces, and non-matching meshes.
  • F4.4.3MOOSE shall compute the heat transfer across small gaps for supported FEM orders and quadratures, moving interfaces, and non-matching meshes.
  • F4.4.4MOOSE shall compute the heat transfer across small gaps for supported FEM orders and quadratures, moving interfaces, and non-matching meshes.
  • F4.4.5MOOSE shall compute the heat transfer across small gaps for supported FEM orders and quadratures, moving interfaces, and non-matching meshes.
